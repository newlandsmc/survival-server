#Claiming
Protect your home by claiming land with GriefPrevention!
&0 
&cWarning: unclaimed land is griefable!
&0 
You can claim up to 1,000 blocks initially. You get 1,000 more claim blocks every mining level!
&0 
You can create up to 3 claims. (You can increase this limit at our donation store!)
&0 
&lCREATING A NEW CLAIM:
&0 
With any golden shovel, right click 2 opposite corners of a rectangular area. This will claim the area between these 2 points.
&0 
Claims automatically extend vertically! You do not need to worry about claiming underground, or the height of your build.
&0 
&lMODIFYING A CLAIM:
&0 
With any golden shovel, right click an existing corner of a claim and then right click the new location you want to move that corner to.
&0 
&lVIEWING A CLAIM:
&0 
You can find the existing corners by right clicking with any stick within your claim. This will make the game show the perimeter of your claim.
&0 
&lMORE HELP:
&0 
There is a lot more you can do with GriefPrevention! You can trust others, split a claim into plots, and more.
